Finger Rules Explained

 Finger rules are used to prevent unnecessary interruptions in meetings. They are automatically sorted by their priority orders so that any questions or replies are handled in a timely manner.
Example: It is important to handle all replies to a current topic first, before moving on to the next. That is why REPLY has a higher priority than NEW TOPIC . Similarly a TECHNICAL REMARK should be handled before any further new topics or replies.
